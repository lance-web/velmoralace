Frequently Asked

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

+

Our standard MOQ is flexible and depends on the style and customization level. We welcome small trial orders for new partners — contact us for a tailored quote.

Can I order a sample before bulk production?

+

Absolutely. We provide pre-production samples for your approval. Sampling typically takes 5-7 days before we proceed to bulk.

What is your typical production lead time?

+

Sampling: 5-7 days. Bulk production: 15-25 days depending on order quantity and complexity.

Do you offer full OEM / ODM services?

+

Yes — from custom logos, labels and fabrics to complete style development and private-label packaging. Your brand, our craftsmanship.

Do you ship worldwide and discreetly?

+

We ship to 50+ countries with discreet, confidential packaging and reliable logistics partners (DHL, FedEx, UPS, sea freight).

Can you create custom luxury packaging?

+

Yes. We offer fully custom, gift-ready luxury packaging — boxes, tissue, hang tags and branded inserts to match your brand identity.
